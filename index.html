Frequently Asked Questions

How does Fynera AI match suppliers and buyers?

Our AI analyzes real-time data including location, product type, quality, quantity, and pricing preferences to intelligently connect the right suppliers with the right buyers—reducing waste and improving efficiency.

Is there a cost to join the platform?

Signing up is free for all user types. We offer tiered subscription plans based on usage volume and access to premium features like advanced analytics and priority matching.

Can logistics providers integrate their own systems?

Yes! Fynera AI supports API integrations with major TMS and fleet management platforms, allowing seamless coordination of loads, tracking, and invoicing.

What types of agricultural products are supported?

We currently support grains, fruits, vegetables, dairy, and livestock. Our platform is designed to be flexible and will expand to additional categories based on user demand.

How is my data protected?

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We comply with GDPR and other relevant data privacy regulations, and never share your business data with third parties without consent.

Do I need technical expertise to use Fynera AI?

Not at all! Our interface is designed for farmers, buyers, and logistics coordinators—no coding or technical background required. Onboarding support is included for all new users.
